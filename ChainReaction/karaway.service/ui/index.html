How To Play

Only Only solo vs AI games can be played (for now); and there is no Chat. Click 'New' to start a new game. When in a game, you can click 'quit' to end the game, or 'Resign' to stop making manual moves; After clicking resign you have to make one more move. The number of players and board size may be set before creating a new game.

  • between 2 to 7 players may be chosen.
  • Board Size may be from 4x4 to 100x100.
  • Win happens after every player has moved once, and only one player has any squares.
  • A player loses when they have no peices/squares of their own color.

    Peices are placed in grid squares

    Placing at least 1 peice in a grid makes it your color.

    Corner squares explode into the 2 near squares whne there are at least 2 peices.

    Edge squares explode into the 3 near squares whne there are at least 3 peices.

    Center squares explode into the 4 near squares whne there are at least 4 peices.

    If a square explodes, one peices is moved to a nearby square. If the square has no other peices in it, the square will become the color of the square exploding. If the square explodes because a peice was added to it from and explosion, it becomes that peices color. If there are more than or equal peices in the target square as the one that gets added, the square remains the color it was.

    Max PeicesPeices in squareResult

    4 1 becomes new color
